
# RANTU - Agricultural Marketplace

**RANTU** is a comprehensive digital platform designed to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers. By cutting out the middlemen, RANTU ensures farmers get fair prices for their harvest while consumers enjoy fresh, high-quality produce at affordable rates.

The platform integrates **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** to empower farmers with market insights and price predictions, while providing buyers with a smart assistant for agricultural knowledge.

## üåü Key Features

### For Buyers (Consumers)
*   **Fresh Marketplace:** Browse a wide catalog of fresh vegetables, fruits, grains, and spices sourced directly from local farms.
*   **Smart Search & Filters:** Easily find products by category, price, or specific keywords.
*   **AI & Seller Chat:** 
    *   **AI Assistant:** Ask about farming tips, weather, or seasonal produce.
    *   **Seller Chat:** Negotiate or ask regarding stock and freshness directly with the farmer.
*   **Seamless Checkout:** Integrated cart, shipping address management (with map pinning), and secure payment simulation.
*   **Real-time Tracking:** Monitor order status and track the courier's location on an interactive map.

### For Sellers (Farmers)
*   **Command Center Dashboard:** Get a bird's-eye view of daily sales, new orders, and active balance.
*   **AI Market Insights:** Receive AI-driven predictions on commodity price trends (e.g., "Chili prices rising by 8%") to adjust pricing strategies.
*   **Inventory Management:** Add, edit, or remove products easily with photo uploads and stock management.
*   **Order Management:** Process incoming orders and update statuses (Waiting, Shipping, Delivered).
*   **Analytics:** Visual charts for sales performance and category distribution.

### Scenario 1: The Health-Conscious Shopper üõí
**Persona:** Sarah, a mother looking for organic ingredients for her family.
1.  **Discovery:** Sarah opens RANTU and sees a "Weekend Harvest Sale" banner.
2.  **Search:** She searches for "Bayam" (Spinach) and filters by "Vegetables".
